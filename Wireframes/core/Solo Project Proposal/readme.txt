                                                Name of the Project : Grocery Manager







        Description: Every get overwelmed when it comes to Grocery, Sometimes you forget something to buy or you don't know how
        much money you need to buy all the Groceries you desire. This app helps you managing your Grocery list so you don't forget
                                     anything any more, and you'll be financially ready.







Features: - You can add new products which you're gonna provide the name, type (fruits, cleaning stuff...), the price
          - You'll have a list of all the products which contains all the informations you provided.
            and a the total price of all the products so you can easily manage the products.
          - You can modify any detail on the products.
          - You can delete any product.
          - You can check on any product once you already bought it or uncheck it (the price of the product will be deducted when checked and added when unchecked) 
          - Your list will remain even when you re-enter.
